💡 Recommendations for Service Improvement

🔹 Expand High‑Demand Services
Check‑up visits represent 30% of all visits. Add two more General Practitioners to reduce wait times and increase capacity. Projected revenue increase: +15% next quarter.
🔹 Marketing for Underutilized Procedures
Injury Treatment (15%) and Dermatology (20%) have room for growth. Launch community outreach about sports medicine and skin cancer screenings.
🔹 Introduce Telehealth Options
Based on 0‑day hospitalization cases (12 patients), virtual follow‑ups can reduce overhead and improve accessibility.
🔹 Budget Allocation & Fee Review
Surgery generates highest revenue per patient ($120). Consider investing in advanced surgical equipment to attract complex cases.

Action plan: Present these proposals at the next Board meeting. Email the board to schedule a discussion.
